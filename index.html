About Visp

Visp is a social media application based around Augmented Reality technology. People may use this application to socialize with other people by attaching texts, animations, pictures, videos, sounds, and even some mini-apps to unique objects, products, and places that they have introduced to the app. After that, they can interact with these previously attached media files shared by users using Visp..

Social media has become one of the most prominent ways in which people spend their time with an estimation of 3.6 billion people using them in 2020. We believe augmented reality technology is an excellent way to enrich the environment around us to be used by people in interactive ways. Our aim in this project was to integrate the perks of augmented reality to a platform of sharing and storing ideas. Our project creates a bridge between the digital world and real-life while allowing people to share an idea with others or save a memory related to a real-life object. Our users can attach texts, videos, images to objects and communicate with creators of the other visps. This is a modern take on social media, which can influence various technologies to be evolved in similar fashion.
